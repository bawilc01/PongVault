What is PongVault?

PongVault is a way to unleash your friendly competitive side during your downtime in a game of ping pong.

How to play PongVault.

  1. You can challenge up to 3 spots ahead of you.
  2. Post challenge in pongvault chat
  3. Loser takes winners rank and vice versa.
  4. You can only challenge the same person once per day.
  5. #1 ranked player is the master of the universe.
  6. After each match the winner must add the results to the challenge page.
